Game autosaves outside of battle
When on the party inventory screen, click on party members to focus them. After, click on items to move them from the party inventory (left) to the person inventory (right) or vice versa.
When on the map screen, click a green square to get back to the party inventory, a blue square to visit a shop, or a redsquare to explore a new room.
New rooms have either: nothing, an item, a party member (limit of 5), or a battle
When you get a new item you have to put it into someone's inventory to use it in battle.

In combat, click on a party member to see their inventory. click one of the items to select it, then click on an enemy (or ally for helpful items) to target it.The person will start charging up, and when they finish they'll use the attack.
